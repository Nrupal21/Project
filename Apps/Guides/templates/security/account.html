Password & Security Tips

Strong Password Tips

  • Use at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid using dictionary words or personal information
  • Use a unique password for each important account
  • Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong passwords

Account Security Best Practices

  • Enable two-factor authentication for all your important accounts
  • Regularly check your account activity for any suspicious logins
  • Update your password every 6-12 months
  • Log out completely when using shared or public computers
