About Our Tiers Program

TravelGuide's rewards tier program is designed to recognize and reward our most loyal customers. As you travel and engage with our services, you'll earn points and climb through our tier levels, unlocking increasingly valuable benefits along the way.

How to Earn and Maintain Tiers

  • Tiers are based on the total points you've earned in the current calendar year.
  • Points are earned through bookings, reviews, referrals, and other activities.
  • Your tier status is recalculated at the beginning of each calendar year.
  • Once you reach a tier, you'll maintain that status for the remainder of the current calendar year and all of the following year.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my tier progress?

You can monitor your current tier status and progress toward the next tier on your Rewards Dashboard.

What happens to my tier if I don't earn enough points?

If you don't earn enough points to maintain your current tier level by the end of the calendar year, you'll be moved to the appropriate tier based on your points total for that year.

Do my points expire?

Points expire 24 months after they are earned if not redeemed. However, tier qualification is based on points earned during the calendar year regardless of whether they have been redeemed or expired.

Can I gift my tier benefits to someone else?

Some benefits, like companion discounts, inherently extend to others. However, most tier benefits are non-transferable and linked to your account.
